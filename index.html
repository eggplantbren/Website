Welcome

Hello and thanks for dropping by. I am a statistician and senior lecturer working at the University of Auckland in New Zealand. I research Bayesian inference in the MaxEnt tradition. On this website you can find information about my research as well as about some other activities and interests.

This is a personal website. Even though much of the content is work-related, nothing on it has been endorsed by my employer.
