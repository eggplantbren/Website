1.5.1 The Metropolis Algorithm
The Metropolis-Hastings algorithm, also known as the Metropolis algorithm,
is the oldest and most fundamental MCMC algorithm. It is quite straight-
forward to implement, and works well on many problems.
The version of
the Metropolis algorithm presented here is sometimes called
random walk
Metropolis.
More sophisticated choices are possible, but usually require
problem-speciﬁc knowledge.
